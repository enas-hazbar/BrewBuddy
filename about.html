About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of students who share a home and try to keep things simple and calm. We enjoy good drinks and time together and sometimes the fridge becomes confusing. BrewBuddy started because we wanted a home that feels more organized and friendly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help everyone see what we have at home in an easy and natural way. No stress and no confusion. Just a clear view for everyone in the house.

A calmer fridge and a nicer shared home for all of us.

Why We Created BrewBuddy

Drinks disappeared fast and nobody was sure what was left. Instead of guessing we wanted something small and helpful that fits student life. BrewBuddy came from that simple need.

What BrewBuddy Offers

  • Clear view of what is in stock
  • Simple tracking of what is used
  • Helps avoid running out of favorites
  • Shows low stock at the right time
  • Makes shared living easier and fair

BrewBuddy is made by students for students. One shared fridge at a time.
